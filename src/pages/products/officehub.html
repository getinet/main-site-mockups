For the small- to medium-sized business that uses FileMaker, OfficeHub makes running your office space simpler and easier. "...not only did it have to be easy to use, but also work with FileMaker"

Introducing OfficeHub: an office and emergency manager that makes running your office a little smoother.

Beezwax understood that this solution had to fit seamlessly into a person’s daily office flow and so we prototyped a native iOS app in Swift to run on an iPad at the front of our own office. We synced it to our FileMaker-based HR system’s employee database so it would have the current office roster, even without a full-time connection.

FILEMAKER INTEGRATION

Is FileMaker an important part of your business? OfficeHub seamlessly connects and syncs with your FileMaker solution integrating your employee roster a breeze. Its companion FileMaker app helps you to quickly link OfficeHub to your staff database, and if you don’t have a staff database, then we can help.

Power of Swift and iOS

A native iOs app using Swift allows for a beautiful, pleasant, and seamless user experience. A native app would allow connection to other types of HR databases in the future, whereas a FileMaker Go app would allow connections to FileMaker systems. The flexibility to add features and to use all the features of the iPad and iPhone, like the camera, fingerprint readers, microphone, and geolocation were far greater with a native app as well.

SOME HIGHLIGHTS

The Hub of your Office

OfficeHub does all the little things that make running your office a little easier.

Reception Kiosk

OfficeHub serves as your reception kiosk so office guests and employees can check-in and check-out. Employees can schedule their guests and get alerted when they arrive.

Useful Information

With just a glance you'll know when your next train will arrive, and what the local weather and traffic conditions are.

REAL TIME ROSTER

Because OfficeHub knows which office guests and employees have checked-in and checked-out, after a building evacuation your office leader can quickly get a roll-call by going through the roster checklist. As a native iPad app, it doesn't need to be connected to the internet to run.

CUSTOMIZABLE BUILDING EVACUATION CHECKLIST

Does someone have the fire extinguisher? Did you backup all of your data? OfficeHub helps you answer these questions with a built-in customizable checklist.
