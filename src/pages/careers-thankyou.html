    company

    Beezwax develops custom solutions for interesting, innovative and fun clients who range from small non-profits to household name corporations. Every day we get to apply and develop our technical skills in multiple problem domains. Our team is largely based in the Bay Area, and partly geographically distributed; wherever we are, we support each other closely with pair programming (when desired) and strong and frequent communication. We have robust code review diligence that emphasizes testing, quality and smell-free code — we have some very technical clients who demand this of us, which is our good luck.

    position

    We’re looking to add a senior Rails developer with strong communication and client skills, who can take on a leadership role both technically and as a mentor to others. You should be actively engaged and passionate about the technologies you are using, quick to learn, and excited to collaborate. The right person for this role is inspired by a work environment in which requirements and technologies vary from project to project, driven by a balance of the client’s needs and our expertise and experience.

    requirements

    • Minimum 3, preferably 6 or more, years professional experience with Rails
    • Thorough knowledge of Ruby on Rails and community standards code around structure and testing, with a commitment to writing well-tested code
    • Communication skills that excel not only with other engineers of all levels, but also with project management and clients of all backgrounds and technical skill
    • Expertise with technical leadership and mentorship
    • Experience working as part of a distributed team

    benefits

    • Competitive compensation, including a retirement plan and an excellent healthcare package with vision and dental
    • Developer productivity and continued learning and professional development. We are active in the developer communities, both online and at events and conferences. We have continuous, vibrant in-house discussion on best practices and are constantly improving one another’s skillsets.
    • Supportive, positive environment with first class project managers

    You can read more about what makes Beezwax different here, with the note that due to a specific client need, this position is located in The Bay Area and is not, initially, available for remote work.

  • technical project manager

    location

    SF Bay Area

    company

    Beezwax is a software development agency based in Oakland, with developers located across the Bay Area and beyond. We’ve been in business for 20 years translating client needs into powerful and elegant web, mobile, and database applications. With over 500 clients so far, we’re continually bringing in new projects that challenge our developers to use the full range of their technical abilities and expand their skill sets. We seek out super-smart people with excellent communication skills and proven rock-solid reliability. Beezwax is a highly collaborative and creative work environment that keeps work interesting and engaging.

    position

    We are looking for a highly competent project manager, communicator, and problem solver who is familiar with the dynamics of a small software development/technical consulting firm.
    The ideal applicant will be able to work with a minimum of structure and supervision, and be able to thrive in a creative, entrepreneurial company.

    duties

    • serving as bridge between developer and client: facilitating communication, anticipating possible problems before they occur
    • working on custom software development projects with a dynamic, growing team of software developers; this job is a combination of collaboration with and management of our development teams, both local and remote
    • maintaining overview of several simultaneous projects for different clients; being accountable to track budget and development progress
    • getting new projects rolling, including discussions with potential clients, collaborating with developers on needs assessment, project scoping, estimating, timeline creation and proposal creation
    • maintaining projects including track budget and development process, and identifying and managing change requests
    • participating with the management team in strategic planning of company-wide infrastructural improvements and long-term objectives

    requirements

    • ability to anticipate the needs of all parties involved in a project
    • excellent written and oral communication skills
    • meticulous attention to detail
    • ability to identify problems and propose solutions with consideration of all ramifications
    • willingness to commute occasionally to clients around the Bay Area
    • experience with agile development methodology is a plus
    • familiarity helping run and grow a business is valuable
    • familiarity with the needs of different types of organizations and businesses is valuable, as we have clients of all sizes in various industries
    • hands-on experience with web development, mobile apps, or FileMaker Pro is a plus
    • 5 years of relevant experience as project manager, developer and/or consultant

    benefits

    • Competitive compensation, including a retirement plan and an excellent healthcare package with vision and dental
    • Beautiful office full of friendly people and tasty snacks in the center of Oakland (right by BART), with excellent restaurants mere blocks in every direction
    • But if you get tired of the office, work remotely whenever you want
    • Support for developer productivity and continued learning in the forms of hardware, software, learning materials, training, and conferences

    We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity at our company. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, religion, color, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, age, marital status, veteran status, or disability status.

    Actual candidates only; no recruiters. No calls please.
