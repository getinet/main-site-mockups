MEETUP DETAILS

It’s a great time to create custom apps. Mobile devices have changed how businesses work. Teams are demanding apps that work across all of their devices. Internal IT organizations can’t deliver them fast enough. This creates a huge opportunity for developers to help businesses create and deploy the custom apps they need.

This Meetup is sponsored by badge badge FileMaker, Inc., an Apple subsidiary, and includes a technical presentation by Beezwax, a FileMaker Business Alliance Platinum Partner.

At this Meetup, you will:

  • Learn how developers are seeing the competitive advantages of new tools that allow:
    1. “Low Code” cloud-ready rapid development of mobile apps for business users, and...
    2. Highly-custom, robust, cross-platform solutions by professional developers.
  • See examples of these two approaches to building solutions for iOS, Mac, Windows, and Web, and how FileMaker enables developers to deliver Mobility, Flexibility, Scalability, and Agility.
  • Network with other business users and professional developers, as well as representatives from FileMaker Inc., an Apple Subsidiary and FileMaker Business Alliance partners, plus staff and software engineering students from 42 Silicon Valley.
  • Hear brief news about the unique partnership between FileMaker and 42 Silicon Valley, to develop the next generation of custom app developers, and the opportunities this partnership is building for training software engineers and in IT recruiting.
