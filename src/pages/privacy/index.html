Beezwax’s Privacy Policy

If any provision of this Privacy Policy is inconsistent or conflicts with any provision of the TOS, this Privacy Policy will control with respect to the subject matter of this Privacy Policy. Capitalized terms used in this Privacy Policy but not defined in this Privacy Policy have the meaning given to them in the TOS.

BY ACCESSING OR VIEWING THE SITE YOU AGREE TO THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY AND YOU EXPRESSLY CONSENT TO THE COLLECTION, USE AND DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION, INCLUDING PERSONAL INFORMATION (AS DEFINED IN SECTION 1.1 BELOW), IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS PRIVACY POLICY. You understand that by providing us Personal Information, you allow us to identify you. You may choose to not provide us with certain information, but some portions of the Site or Beezwax Content may not be available or may be limited as a result. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, you should immediately cease use of the Site.

  1. Types of Information. We collect both Personal Information and Anonymous Information, each as defined below, in connection with your use of the Site and the content on the site. Information that is not Personal Information or Anonymous Information is governed by the TOS.
    1. Personal Information. “Personal Information” is information which may be used to identify or contact an individual, such as your name, mailing address, phone number, or email address. You may be asked to provide this information when registering for an account on the Site or when communicating with us in connection with the Site or content on the Site.
    2. Anonymous Information.“Anonymous Information” is information we collect in connection with your use of the Site, and the content on the Site, that is not associated with or linked to your Personal Information and which, on its own, does not individually identify you.
  2. How We Collect and Use Information.
    1. Information You Provide. You may provide us Personal Information to determine the suitability of products and services referenced on the Site or to register an account. Consistent with this Privacy Policy, we may use or share your Personal Information for completing a transaction, performing some action you have requested or communicating with you. We use your registration information to set up, maintain, and track your account. We may also internally use or combine your Personal Information with other information to improve our products and services, and for advertising and marketing consistent with this Privacy Policy, such as announcements, newsletters and special offers from Beezwax.
    2. Information Collected Automatically When Visiting the Site. We use “cookies” to collect Anonymous Information about visitors to the Site. We may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to identify that you’ve logged in to the Site and to tell us how and when you interact with our Site. We may use, transfer, and disclose this Anonymous Information for any purpose such as improving the experience for visitors to our Site, customizing the Site for individual visitors, and marketing. Although most browsers automatically accept cookies, you can change your browser options to stop automatically accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting cookies.
    3. Web Beacons. Pages within the Site and our e-mails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags and single-pixel gifs) that permit us, for example, to count users who have visited those pages or opened an e-mail and for other related Site statistics (for example, recording the popularity of certain content and verifying system and server integrity). This information is used to enable more accurate reporting, improve the effectiveness of our marketing, and improve the Site.
    4. Anonymous Information Generally. We may create Anonymous Information records from Personal Information by excluding information (such as your name) that would otherwise make such data personally identifiable to you. We may use Anonymous Information at our discretion and for any purpose.
    5. Case Studies and Blog Posts. In certain circumstances we provide case studies and blog posts which contain information we have learned through unique experiences. In circumstances where we would like to use Personal Information, such as a name which may identify you, we will not do so unless we have your permission.
